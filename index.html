Summer 2025 - Camino de Santiago

This is a 1500km pilgrimage from Le Puy-en-Velay, France to Santiago de Compostela, Spain. The trek should take 6-7 weeks. Trekkers (in the thousands!) stay at churches, hostels, or camp along the way. Most people choose shorter routes but I will complete the longest route for the lulz.
